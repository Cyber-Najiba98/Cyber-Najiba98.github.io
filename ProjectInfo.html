Projet Description



Parenting is an important part of life in a family structure. Parent engagement is a term used to describe parents' involvement in all aspects of their children's education and development, based on the assumption that parents are their children's primary influence. Having a child is similar to relocating to a new nation in many respects. The joys of parenthood are matched by the seemingly unending changes you must make in your lifestyle and mindset.

New mothers must learn to balance the responsibilities of nursing and nurturing their child while also caring for themselves.New fathers must learn when and how to provide a helping hand to their children.
It takes time for both parents to adjust to the new environment. Despite that most parents eventually learn to adapt to their new positions, some face very difficult times along the way, which leads to making mistakes that could hurt the child and/or the spouse. This is where our app’s aim comes in hand, to minimize the damage whether it's on the relationship between the parent and the child or between parents themselves
